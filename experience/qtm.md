Through the QTM Math Circle at Emory University, I not only learned about complex mathematics topics (Brachistochrones, Number Theory, Statistics, etc.) in mathemtaics, but also conducted research in Extremal Graph Theory.

<br>

Under my mentor, Dr. Bradley Elliott (Emory University), my research partner and I studied the Turan Number for K-Chorded cycles. We presented the research to 20 math faculty @ Emory and 46 undergrad and grad students.
