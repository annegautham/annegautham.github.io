I'm a founder and the CTO of <a href="https://annegautham.github.io/ivyrootsconsulting/">Ivy Roots Consulting</a>, a nonprofit organization that provides free essay review, college consulting, and SAT preperation for underpriveledged and low-income students.

<br>

 Unfortunately, low-income communities may not have access to application advice resources. So, Ivy Roots Consulting is committed to providing students with the tools and support they need to apply and attend college. We help students create a successful college application strategy and guides them through the college admissions process.
