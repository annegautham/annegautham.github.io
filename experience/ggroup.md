I founded and am the current CEO of <a href="https://thegaussgroup.org">The Gauss Group</a> , a nonprofit organization dedicted to engaging underprivileged students with math typically not taught in their schools. So far, we have taught over 15 courses to 120+ students, held 3 math tournaments, and are sponsored by companies such as Wolfram.

<br>

As an instructor, I've taught middle and high schoolers in Geometry, Number Theory, Advanced Algebra (Introductory Linear Algebra), Multivariable Calculus, Differential Equations, Combinatorics, and Probablity. I also hosted office hours (Monday-Friday, 6-7 PM CST) for students to check in and receive feedback on problem sets.

<br>

I'm the primary front and back-end developer of the website.
