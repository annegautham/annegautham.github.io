I'm making a LiDAR based drone that can fly around and map the environment! Here's the hardware so far:

<br>

* 1500mAh 3S 20C LiPo Battery + XT60 Connector + Battery Monitor
* PDB (Power Distribution Board)
* UBEC (Universal Battery Eliminator Circuit)
* Raspberry Pi Model 4 Model B + Pi Hat PCB + Heatsinks (no burnouts on my watch)
* ToF Sensor (LiDAR for measuring altitude)
* SLAMTEC's RPLiDAR A1 for SLAM
* DX2205 BLDC Motors + 30A ESCs (Electronic Speed Controllers)
* SP Racing F3 Flight Controller Acro
* RPi Camera Module for Optical Flow measurements (planar position + velocity)
* Carbon Fiber Frame + 3 bladed Propellors
