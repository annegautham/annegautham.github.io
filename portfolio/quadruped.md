To apply the knowledge I acquired through the Coursera Northwestern & UPenn Robotics Specializations, I decided to develop a quadruped! The robot consisted of a laser-cut acrylic frame and 12 hobby servos (4 per leg, controlling 3 degrees of motion). I also built a little remote with a wireless transeiver to control the quadruped.
