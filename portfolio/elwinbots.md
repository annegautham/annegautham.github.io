Under the advisorship of Professor Matthew Elwin (Assistant Professor of Instruction of Mechanical Engineering, Center for Robotics & Biosystems), I'm designing and developing, from scratch, cost-efficient swarm bots tasked with moving heavy payloads. We take inspiration from the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BOxlpYYsgmE">Omnid Mobile Manipulators</a>, an experimential platform designed for testing autonomous multirobot cooperative manipulation and human-collaborative multirobot manipulation.
